2.1 Local Data Storage (Your Device Only)

The following information is stored exclusively on your device and never transmitted to our servers:

  • Ingredient Inventory: Your pantry contents, quantities, and expiration dates
  • Dietary Preferences: Vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, keto, paleo, dairy-free settings
  • Food Allergies: Nuts, shellfish, eggs, soy, fish, sesame, sulfites, lactose intolerances
  • Cooking Preferences: Preferred cooking times, serving sizes, cuisine types, difficulty levels
  • Recipe History: Generated recipes, ratings, and feedback
  • Saved Recipes: Your favorite recipes and cooking notes
  • Usage Analytics: Local analytics for pantry optimization (never transmitted)
  • App Settings: Theme preferences, notification settings, and app configuration

2.2 Advertising Data Collection

Google AdMob Integration: To support our free app, we use Google AdMob for advertising. When you consent to personalized ads, the following data may be collected:

Device Information:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Screen size and resolution
  • Mobile advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address (for general location, not precise location)

App Usage Data:

  • Ad interaction data (clicks, impressions, time spent viewing)
  • General app usage patterns (session duration, feature usage frequency)
  • Crash reports and performance metrics

Your Control:

  • Opt-Out Anytime: Disable personalized ads in app settings

  • Non-Personalized Ads: Choose to see generic ads instead of personalized ones
  • Consent Management: Your choices are remembered and respected
