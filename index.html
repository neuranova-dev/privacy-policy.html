2. How Your Data is Handled

Local Storage Only: Everything you enter in AI Pantry Chef is stored exclusively on your device using Android's secure local storage systems. We cannot access this data.

2.1 What Information You Store Locally

The following information is stored only on your device to provide personalized recipe recommendations:

  • Ingredient Inventory: Your pantry contents and expiration dates
  • Dietary Preferences: Vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, keto, paleo settings
  • Food Allergies & Intolerances: Nuts, shellfish, eggs, soy, fish, sesame, sulfites, lactose
  • Liked/Disliked Ingredients: Your personal taste preferences
  • Cooking Preferences: Preferred cooking time, serving sizes, cuisine types, difficulty levels
  • Shopping Lists: Items you want to purchase
  • Recipe Feedback: Your ratings and feedback on generated recipes
  • Saved Recipes: Recipes you choose to save for future reference (stored in local SharedPreferences)
  • Usage Patterns: Recipe generation history (for improving recommendations)

2.2 Local Storage Methods

  • Android SharedPreferences: User preferences, settings, and saved recipes (encrypted)
  • Room/SQLite Database: Recipe data, pantry inventory, shopping lists (local database)
  • Internal Storage: App assets and temporary files
